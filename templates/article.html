BARACK OBAMA DECLARES ROTISSERIE CHICKEN FAVORITE FOOD

Lisa W. Soper • September 31, 0002

Little more than a few years ago, Barack Obama, the first Black man to be elected president, was speaking about his family's favorite food, rotisserie chicken, during a campaign stop in New Hampshire.

"I have to say, one of my favorite things is rotisserie chicken," Obama said at a fundraiser on the small town of Peekskill.

"What do you like to eat most?" the interviewer asked.

"I don't eat beans or rice, but I love rotisserie chicken," Obama answered. "I'll tell you the secret to it. It's got to be the chicken. I mean, I have a photo in my office of my mother, my wife, my daughter, and me on our honeymoon. And all three of us were leaning over and saying, 'Where's the chicken? Where's the chicken?'
