YOUNG MAN CONVINCED WORLD RENOWNED TOOTHPASTE SALESMAN TO KILL HIMSELF THIS EVENING


Juice Derb • Last Yesterday

A young man has convinced world renowned Toothpaste Salesman to kill himself this evening.

After a terrible night's sleep, the young man, who wishes to remain anonymous, decides to kill himself. After receiving a call from the Toothpaste Salesman, the young man wakes up. He goes to his room and removes the toothpaste from his toothbrush, then goes to the window. The Toothpaste Salesman calls the young man back into his office. The young man begins to say that he wants to be happy, but the salesman is not having it. The young man is shocked by the salesman's attitude.

It is a sad day for the poor girl who was being treated at the dental clinic. She will need to be examined by a dentist.

"Good morning. Did you have a good sleep last night? I saw a lot of tears in your eyes." The dentist asks.

"Well, I was worried because I heard the toothpaste salesman was treating you bad."

"Why? What did I do?" The dental office worker asks.

"I will see you in the exam room soon." The dentist says and disappears inside the office.

The girl is nervous.
