SCIENTISTS DISCOVER NEW FISH OFF COAST OF SWITZERLAND


By Chamlin Clam • Multiple days ago

Off the coast of Switzerland, a new fish was discovered, dubbed the "Spank the Daddy!" because it is so aggressive that a fisherman has to spank it to calm it down.

The fish, named the "Vallolato" or "Spank the Daddy" by fisherman Marco Tomilla to describe his wife's aggressive behavior, was first thought to be a large bluefin tuna.

"I was shocked when I came back to the ship because I thought the fish was a large bluefin tuna and it was not," Marco Tomilla, a fisherman on the island of Chaudiere, in the southern part of the Sargasso Sea, said to a news release. "The animal was huge and it hit the boat with a terrible noise."

A group of five fishermen, led by Marco Tomilla, had to take the animal back to port, but luckily the animal did not harm anyone.
