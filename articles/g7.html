IRISH COMPANIES CLAIM PRESIDENT TRUMPS “LLAMA MILITARY FUND INITIATIVE” SOLD THEM DEFECTIVE LLAMAS.


By George G. Schutte • 69 hours ago

Copyright NEWS TROOP, 2019

President Trump recently sold a herd of llamas to an Irish company, in order to fund a new military base. The company claims the llamas are “Horribly malformed” and “an affront to nature” .

Selling such a rare breed is illegal. This is not the first time the Trump family has been implicated in animal abuse, as President Trump recently paid over $25,000 to a lobbyist who works for a company who is accused of selling fake dogs to Mexican slaughterhouse workers.

The spokesman of the company has said that “While $2.55 for an entire herd is cheap, these are less like llamas, and more like deformed, bloodthirsty brachycephalic goats “ that are extremely likely to be bred specifically for a profit.

'It's kind of like selling dog bones to a Nazi' he told a reporter on the phone in response to the Trump campaign's charge that he is exploiting the animals.
